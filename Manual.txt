Commands:
up, down, right, left --> Control the player to move towards that direction for 1 unit
goto <x> <y> --> Go to the position (x, y)
check --> Check the place around you
pick --> Pick up the things around you, if there's something
inv check --> Check your inventory
inv discard <id> <amount> --> Discard the amount of items in your inventory
plant <id> --> Plant a specific kind of seed right here

Plants:
Wheat --> 10s
Potato --> 16s
Corn --> 20s
Grass --> 5s

Item ID:
0 -> Wheat
1 -> Potato
2 -> Corn
3 -> Grass
